Narratives and Context: A Study into the Nature of NBA Basketball

Our list includes:

  1. Creator Opinion
    • My own opinion of the player
  2. Possession Time
    • Player TOP/Team TOP
  3. No. of Touches
    • No. of Touches he gets/Team touches OVR (+ whether it is a Shot or a Pass (Shot/OVR Touches)
  4. Shots
    • % of his shots in games.
  5. Shot Selection
    • FG% (decimals) + Shot Selection + % of Each Spot weighted by frequency (Most shots in one spot = more points for that spot
  6. Heat Map
    • Physical representation of heat map of player
  7. Off Ball Movement
    • No. of repossessions of the ball after passing + ability to get open (ft of separation)
  8. Matchup quality
    • Avg. Matchup of Defender the player gets during the game based on defensive percentile.
  9. Team Situation
    • Prior Knowledge of the Team (Overall rating within team).
  10. Coaching Scheme
    • Type of plays + How well they fit the player (ex. if a player is tall and they run plays outside the key).
  11. BPM+
    • BPM + (TOs + Passes that the team does with him off the floor).
  12. Pace
    • Miles ran/Avg. Miles Ran in normal games or steps taken
  13. Control
    • TOV% + Shot Selection (FG% of spots + Frequency Metric)
  14. Quarterly Performance
    • Evaluation of performance by each quarter
  15. EOG Stats (Counting Stats)
    • Regular stats at the end of the game

There are linked Criteriae that show what is being tracked more strictly and what requires more depth and research to get a more final result.
